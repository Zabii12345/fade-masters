FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment? −

No! Walk-ins are always welcome. You can also book online to avoid waiting times.

Do you do kids' haircuts? +

Yes, we offer professional and gentle haircuts for children starting at 12€.

What services do you offer? +

We offer men's & women's haircuts, beard trimming, coloring, highlights, perms, waxing, and more.

How long does a haircut take? +

Most haircuts take between 20-45 minutes depending on the service.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept cash, card, and contactless payments.

What are your opening hours? +

Mon–Sat: 09:00 AM–08:00 PM. Sunday closed.

Where is the salon located? +

Turmstraße 21, 10559 Berlin Moabit.

Do you offer hair coloring? +

Yes, we offer root color, full color, highlights, balayage and more.
